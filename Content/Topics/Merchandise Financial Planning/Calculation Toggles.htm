Calculation Toggles

The Calculation Toggles pane defines which dependent metrics recalculate and which ones hold when changes are made:

  • Calculation toggles do not require saving to be in effect. The change is immediate one the selection is made.

  • When alternate (non-default) calc toggles are selected, they appear in darker gray for easy identification at a glance

  • The Calc Toggles view can be collapsed by clicking the arrow at the top left adjacent the view title:



    Once collapsed, the view will be visible only as a tab at the right hand side of the screen and can be restored by clicking the arrow near the view title again:



  • Note: Not all plannable metrics have multiple calculation directions, thus not all plannable metrics are shown in this view
  • Note: Based on the planning method – Retail or Cost – some calculation toggles may not be available. This is due to the differences in how the calculation model must function together as a whole to properly balance within each method
